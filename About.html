About us

Over the years, St. Paul’s Catholic Mission Day Primary School gained a reputation for academic excellence
and a commitment to holistic education, nurturing students not only academically but also spiritually, emotionally, and socially.
The school became a beacon of hope and opportunity for many students, providing them with a supportive and conducive learning environment
where they could thrive and reach their full potential. In 2017, another pivotal moment in the school’s history occurred when it was upgraded to a secondary school,
offering a comprehensive secondary education curriculum to students up to grade 12. This upgrade was a testament to the school’s continued growth and success,
reflecting its commitment to providing quality education and preparing students for higher education and future careers.
